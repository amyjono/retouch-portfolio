About Me

I'm a freelance retoucher based in Norfolk and I have been retouching professionally since 2010. I have a strong eye for colour correction and I specialise in fashion and product retouching with an interest for fine jewellery. I have a wide range of retouching experience including e-commerce, editorial and beauty.

In my spare time I love coding, cycling, skiing in the winter and pretzels when I need a snack.
